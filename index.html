About Us

We are a non- profit charitable organization (501(c)3), founded on April 12, 2012. Our mission is to assist Law Enforcement officers & immediate families in times of need from unforeseen emergencies. Every day men and women in blue risk their lives to make our community safer. We provide assistance to unforeseen events related to “On Duty” illness or death. In the case of an “Off Duty” illness and injuries are reviewed on a case by case basis. Our assistance is provided to all police officers, regardless of their rank, background, or race. In addition to our cause, we gladly take requests for any assistance under advisement from any Law Enforcement Officer, Detention Officer, Corrections Officer, or Dispatcher regardless of their union affiliation, per the guidelines of our written Corporate Bylaws. We are able to continue our purpose because of the support and generosity of the individual and business donors. We aim to closely connect to the community by organizing special outreach events that keep us in touch with the people we serve.
